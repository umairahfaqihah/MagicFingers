About Magic Fingers

Magic Fingers is an innovative educational platform that combines cutting-edge AI hand-gesture recognition powered by Google's MediaPipe with engaging math games designed for children aged 3–8.

Our mission: make math feel like magic. Children learn best when they're having fun — so we built a world where every correct answer earns a star, every level brings new challenges, and every session builds real math skills.
